FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

This section provides quick answers to common questions about SIDLAK KAALAM, an offline HTML-based instructional material for Grade 10 Earth and Space Science. Whether you're a teacher or student, these FAQs will help you navigate and use the material effectively.

What is SIDLAK KAALAM? ▶

SIDLAK KAALAM is an offline HTML-based instructional material designed for teaching key concepts in Earth and Space Science. It integrates interactive content, visuals, and activities aligned with the Most Essential Learning Competencies (MELCs) of the K to 12 Science Curriculum.

Do I need an internet connection to use SIDLAK KAALAM? ▶

No. SIDLAK KAALAM is designed to work offline. Once copied or installed on your computer or USB device, you can access it through any web browser without an internet connection.

How do I open the instructional material? ▶

Simply double-click the file named sidlak.html inside the SIDLAK KAALAM folder. It will open in your default web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Edge).

Can I use SIDLAK KAALAM on a mobile device or tablet? ▶

SIDLAK KAALAM is optimized for desktop and laptop browsers. While some features may work on mobile devices, the best experience is through a computer or laptop.

What grade level is this material intended for? ▶

SIDLAK KAALAM is developed for Grade 10 students studying Earth and Space Science during the first quarter of the school year.

Is SIDLAK KAALAM aligned with the DepEd curriculum? ▶

Yes. The content is strictly aligned with the Most Essential Learning Competencies (MELCs) set by the Department of Education for Grade 10 Science.

What types of learning activities are included? ▶

SIDLAK KAALAM includes:

  • Concept animations and diagrams
  • Videos
  • Practice quizzes
  • Guided inquiry tasks
  • Real-life application problems
  • Assessments with answer keys

What should I do if the material doesn’t load properly? ▶

Make sure:

  • You're using a modern web browser (updated Chrome or Firefox)
  • You opened the sidlak.html file from the local folder
  • All folder contents remain intact and in the same directory
If issues persist, contact the developer.

Can SIDLAK KAALAM be used for remote or modular learning? ▶

Yes. Since it works offline, it is suitable for modular learning setups, especially in areas with limited or no internet access. It can be distributed via USB, CD, or offline file-sharing platforms.

Is the material interactive even if it's offline? ▶

Yes. SIDLAK KAALAM includes interactive quizzes, animations, and clickable content—all functional without internet as long as it’s accessed through a browser.

Who developed SIDLAK KAALAM? ▶

This instructional material was developed by Leomel Amparado as part of a graduate thesis titled “Construction and Validation of Offline Web-based Instructional Material for Teaching Earth and Space Science.”

How do I check my understanding of the topics? ▶

Each lesson includes interactive quizzes and self-assessment activities. You can take these after completing a topic to test your knowledge. The results are provided instantly for your feedback.

Is the information presented here reliable? ▶

Yes. The content in SIDLAK KAALAM has been compiled and validated from credible academic sources, including textbooks, scientific journals, and reputable educational websites. A list of references is available in the "Reference" section.

How can teachers integrate SIDLAK KAALAM into their lesson plans? ▶

Teachers can use SIDLAK KAALAM as a supplementary material during discussions, or assign it for independent learning. The structure follows the MELCs, making it easy to align with daily lesson logs (DLLs) and classroom-based assessments.

I think I found an error in the content. What should I do? ▶

We appreciate your feedback! If you find any factual errors or typos, please inform the project developer at main.23002268@cnu.edu.ph. Your input helps in improving the quality of this learning material for everyone
