About The Innovator

LEOMEL A. AMPARADO, MAEd (cand), LPT.

Mr. Leomel A. Amparado is a Licensed Professional Teacher with a Bachelor of Secondary Education degree, majoring in General Science, from Biliran Province State University (BiPSU). He is currently pursuing a Master of Arts in Education, major in Science Education, at Cebu Normal University, where he continues to enhance his knowledge and pedagogical skills in science instruction.

A scholar of the Department of Science and Technology`s (DOST) Capacity Building Program for Science and Mathematics Education, Mr. Amparado is committed to advancing science education in the Philippines. This prestigious scholarship recognizes his academic potential and his dedication to improving the quality of science teaching and learning in the country.

His early foundation in the sciences was shaped during his secondary education at the Naval State University – Laboratory High School under the Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) strand. He further honed his instructional skills through a comprehensive internship at the Naval School of Fisheries, where he taught under the Science, Technology, and Engineering Program (STEP).

Mr. Amparado also holds Honor Graduate Eligibility under Presidential Decree 907 and is a licensed professional teacher accredited by the Professional Regulation Commission.

This instructional material is a product of his vision to create meaningful, technology-integrated learning experiences in Earth and Space Science. It reflects his mission to inspire learners and support educators by making science engaging, relevant, and accessible.
